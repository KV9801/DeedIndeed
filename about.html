About Us

Welcome to Deed Indeed

DeedIndeed is a social enterprise and online initiative with the goal of increasing the sustainability of NGOs across Mumbai. We accomplish this by providing online resources for NGOs to increase their awareness and access to donors, resources, and skills. We use technology to spread knowledge from our experienced fundraising experts to NGOs around the world and increase their capacity.

OUR GOALS: To help the NGOs and charity giving become more transparent by making their financials available for review and analysis. To help smaller NGOs have the same visibility as larger NGOs and access pools of money or chunks of time that tend to be monopolized by larger NGOs. To help those who wish to donate money and/or time select a cause and charity of their choice to work with. To help NGOs raise their level of growth and evolution by providing the NGOs access to articles and guides on how to be better organized to handle the challenges of growth.
