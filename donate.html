About Us

DeedIndeed is a social enterprise and online initiative with the goal of increasing the sustainability of NGOs across Mumbai. We accomplish this by providing online resources for NGOs to increase their awareness and access to donors, resources, and skills. We use technology to spread knowledge from our experienced fundraising experts to NGOs around the world and increase their capacity.
